Paris

Overview

Paris Paris, the capital and largest city of France, is a global center for art,fashion, gastronomy, and culture. It's known for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral, its grand boulevards, and its vibrant cafe culture. The city offers a blend of historical charm and modern attractions, making it a popular destination for tourists.
